## Forms & CTAs

### Contact Form
- **Location:** /contact page
- **Fields:** Name, Email, Phone, Message
- **Submit:** Sends to pharmacy via SendGrid
- **Confirmation:** "We'll get back to you within 24 hours"

### Appointment Form
- **Location:** /appointments page
- **Fields:** Name, Email, Phone, Service Type, Date, Time, Notes
- **Services:** Consultation, Refill, Flu Shot, COVID Vaccine, Med Review, Health Screening
- **Submit:** Sends to pharmacy
- **Confirmation:** "We'll confirm within 24 hours"

### Refill Form
- **Location:** /refills page
- **Fields:** Name, Email, Phone, Medication, Rx Number, Refills Needed, Notes
- **Submit:** Sends to pharmacy
- **Confirmation:** "We'll update you within 2 hours"
