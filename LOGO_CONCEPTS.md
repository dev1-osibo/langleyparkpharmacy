# Logo Concepts - Langley Park Pharmacy

## Overview
Four professional logo concepts for Langley Park Pharmacy, a community-focused pharmacy in Takoma Park, MD. Each logo incorporates green, blue, and yellow tones on a clean white background.

---

## Logo Concept 1: "Capsule Home"

**Visual Description:**
A minimalist geometric design combining a pill/capsule shape with a house silhouette. The capsule forms the outer shape (horizontal oval), with a small house outline inside. Clean, modern, friendly.

**Colors:**
- Primary: Pharmacy Green (#2D7B4F)
- Accent: White (#FFFFFF)
- Background: Clean white

**Style:**
- Geometric and modern
- Simple line-based design
- Highly recognizable at small sizes
- Works well as favicon

**Best Uses:**
- Website header/logo
- Favicon
- Social media profile picture
- Email signature

**Why It Works:**
Symbolizes the intersection of healthcare (capsule) and community (home). Instantly communicates pharmacy + community care.

---

## Logo Concept 2: "Heartbeat Cross"

**Visual Description:**
A stylized pharmacy cross (+ symbol) integrated with a heartbeat line running through it. The heartbeat creates the top and bottom horizontal bars, while vertical bar remains straight. Modern healthcare symbol with life/wellness message.

**Colors:**
- Primary: Professional Blue (#1E5A96)
- Secondary: Pharmacy Green (#2D7B4F)
- Gradient effect: Blue to green transition
- Background: White

**Style:**
- Medical but approachable
- Dynamic (movement implied by heartbeat)
- Professional and trustworthy
- Bold, memorable

**Best Uses:**
- Main website logo
- Business cards
- Marketing materials
- Print & digital advertising

**Why It Works:**
The heartbeat element adds warmth and life to the traditional pharmacy symbol. Conveys "we care about your health" through the visual metaphor.

---

## Logo Concept 3: "Growth & Care"

**Visual Description:**
A stylized mortar and pestle combined with three growing leaves or plants. The leaves represent growth, wellness, and natural care. The mortar base represents pharmaceutical expertise. Clean, organic lines.

**Colors:**
- Primary: Pharmacy Green (#2D7B4F)
- Accent: Yellow (#F4C430) - highlights on leaves
- Secondary: Light Green (#A8D5BA)
- Background: White

**Style:**
- Nature-inspired
- Holistic healthcare feeling
- Friendly and approachable
- Organic but professional

**Best Uses:**
- Secondary/alternative logo
- Wellness/health initiatives
- Social media graphics
- Blog feature images
- Patient education materials

**Why It Works:**
Appeals to customers interested in natural health and preventive care. Suggests that the pharmacy cares about whole-person wellness, not just medication dispensing.

---

## Logo Concept 4: "Local Pharmacy Pin"

**Visual Description:**
A location/map pin (teardrop shape) containing a medicine bottle silhouette inside. The bottle is centered within the pin. Combines community location element with pharmaceutical symbolism.

**Colors:**
- Primary: Professional Blue (#1E5A96)
- Accent: Yellow (#F4C430) - cap of bottle
- Highlight: Pharmacy Green (#2D7B4F)
- Background: White

**Style:**
- Modern and minimalist
- Emphasizes "local" element
- Tech-forward (map pin)
- Friendly and accessible

**Best Uses:**
- Google Maps listing
- Location badges
- Social media
- Local marketing campaigns
- "Find Us" sections

**Why It Works:**
Highlights that Langley Park Pharmacy is a neighborhood resource. The map pin element is immediately recognizable and emphasizes the "local" aspect of the business.

---

## Recommendation

**Primary Logo:** Concept 2 (Heartbeat Cross)
- Most professional and versatile
- Works at all sizes
- Clearly communicates pharmacy + healthcare
- Emotionally resonant (heartbeat = care)

**Secondary Logo:** Concept 1 (Capsule Home)
- Perfect for favicon and small applications
- Friendly and approachable
- Community focus

**Supporting Logo:** Concept 3 (Growth & Care)
- For wellness initiatives and content
- Blog and educational materials

**Functional Badge:** Concept 4 (Local Pharmacy Pin)
- Google Maps and location-based marketing
- "Find Us" buttons
