## 6. EDITING TIPS FOR FACEBOOK

### When Adding Bio/About:
1. Go to **Settings** → **About**
2. Look for these sections:
   - **"About"** — paste the "About Us" section
   - **"Business Type"** — make sure it says "Pharmacy"
   - **"Phone"** — (301) 557-9418 or (301) 844-5280
   - **"Address"** — 8004 New Hampshire Ave, Takoma Park, MD 20912
   - **"Hours"** — Mon-Fri 9 AM - 7 PM | Sat 10 AM - 3 PM
   - **"Website"** — langleyparkpharmacy.com (or your site)

### When Creating Posts:
1. Click **"Create"** at the top
2. Paste the post text
3. Attach image (if applicable)
4. Add hashtags at the end
5. Click **"Post"**

### When Setting Up Messenger Auto-Reply:
1. Go to **Settings** → **Messaging**
2. Look for **"Instant Reply"** or **"Auto Reply"**
3. Toggle it ON
4. Paste the auto-reply text
5. Save
