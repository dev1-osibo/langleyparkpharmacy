# Flu Season Prep: How to Stay Healthy This Year

**Published:** September 15, 2023

## Get Ready for Flu Season

As the weather gets cooler, flu season approaches. Here's what you need to know to stay healthy.

## Why the Flu Shot Matters

The annual flu vaccine is the best protection against the flu. It's safe, effective, and helps protect both you and vulnerable people in your community.

### Who Should Get Vaccinated?

- Everyone 6 months and older
- Especially people over 65
- People with chronic health conditions
- Pregnant women
- Healthcare workers

## Beyond the Vaccine

While vaccination is your best defense, other healthy habits help too:

1. **Wash your hands frequently** with soap and water for at least 20 seconds
2. **Avoid touching** your face, eyes, and mouth
3. **Get enough sleep** to support your immune system
4. **Stay hydrated** by drinking plenty of water
5. **Exercise regularly** to keep your immune system strong
6. **Eat nutritious foods** rich in vitamins and minerals

## When to Stay Home

If you develop flu-like symptoms (fever, cough, body aches), stay home and contact your doctor. Most people recover in 7-10 days.
