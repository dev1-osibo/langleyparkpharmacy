# Understanding Your Medications: A Guide to Common Questions

**Published:** April 1, 2024

## Introduction

Medications are a crucial part of managing many health conditions, but they can also raise a lot of questions. In this post, we'll answer some of the most common questions our pharmacists hear.

## Frequently Asked Medication Questions

### Should I take my medication with food?

This depends on the specific medication. Some medications work better on an empty stomach, while others may cause nausea and should be taken with food. Always check the label or ask your pharmacist.

### What should I do if I miss a dose?

If you miss a dose, take it as soon as you remember, unless it's almost time for the next dose. Never double up on doses. When in doubt, call your pharmacy.

### Can I drink alcohol while taking medication?

This varies by medication. Some medications have dangerous interactions with alcohol. Always ask your pharmacist before combining alcohol with any medication.

### How do I store my medications properly?

Most medications should be stored at room temperature, away from moisture and heat. Avoid storing medications in the bathroom where humidity is high. Some medications need to be refrigerated – check the label.

## When to Call Your Pharmacist

- Have questions about your medications
- Take supplements or over-the-counter products
- Are concerned about drug interactions
