# Managing Medication Side Effects: What You Need to Know

**Published:** March 20, 2024

## Understanding Medication Side Effects

All medications carry the potential for side effects. The good news is that many side effects are manageable, and our pharmacists can help.

## Common Medication Side Effects

### Nausea
- Try taking your medication with food (if appropriate for your drug)
- Ginger supplements may help
- Contact your pharmacist if nausea is severe

### Dizziness
- Get up slowly from sitting or lying down
- Avoid sudden movements
- Stay hydrated
- Report persistent dizziness to your doctor

### Headaches
- Ensure you're drinking enough water
- Get adequate sleep
- Over-the-counter pain relievers may help
- Consult your pharmacist if headaches persist

### Sleep Issues
- Some medications can interfere with sleep
- Taking medication at a different time of day may help
- Ask your pharmacist about the best time to take your medication

## When to Contact a Healthcare Provider

Seek immediate medical attention if you experience:
- Severe allergic reactions (difficulty breathing, swelling)
- Chest pain or difficulty breathing
- Severe headache
- Loss of consciousness
- Any symptom that concerns you

## Prevention and Management

1. **Take medication exactly as prescribed** – changes in dosage or timing can affect side effects
2. **Keep a medication journal** to track any symptoms
3. **Communicate with your pharmacist** – we can adjust timing or suggest alternatives
4. **Don't stop taking medication** without consulting your doctor
5. **Ask about side effect risks** before starting a new medication
